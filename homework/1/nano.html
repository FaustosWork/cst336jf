Nano-Computers

Everyone has seen Iron Man at one point or another. If you haven't, you should definitely check it out since it may give a very large spoiler of what the future can have. The ARC reactor, the device that gave Iron Man the ability to continue staying alive despite shrapnel being severly close to his heart is an example of something that can be achieved with Nano-Computing.

Nano-Computing is when the actual dimensions of a computer are small enough to only be seen through a microscope. Like Iron Man's Arc Reactor, there are several beneficial ideas and types of Nano-Computers that we can see developed within the next couple of years. One popular idea is swallowing a pill that contains a nano-computer with the idea that if you're sick, the nano-computer will be able to find and eliminate the illness by telling the user what is wrong and how to fix it. This will only be truly possible with Computer Science's beast: the Quantum Computer.
